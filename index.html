Description

1 in 8 Women is diagnosed with breast cancer in their lifetime, and 26.7% of them report delays in wait time, this project aims to reduce the wait times during diagnoses of high risk cases, using AI.

Solving the problem

We are trying to reduce the time it takes for a person with an uncertain case to be diagnosed with a malignant tumor and start their treatment, since this is a very lengthy process in the third world public health system.

Making human life better

We are helping by reducing the number of deaths produced by delayed testing, as well as lowering overall stress levels for women and men during their breast cancer diagnosis.

Solution methodology

This program will allow medical practitioners and radiological technicians to diagnose tumors with more ease by the use of a free online program that uses Convolutional Neural Networks to assess a tumor, right now it has an accuracy of 50%, and it can be improved with more training time, also when ever a result is uncertain the program shows it.

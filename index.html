Description

Women in the U.S. have a 1 in 8 (or about 13 percent) lifetime risk of getting breast cancer. About 31.7% of them report delays in the waiting time to obtain a diagnosis, this project aims to reduce the waiting time of high-risk cases, using a convolutional neural network (CNN) and a large sample of mammography scans to train the model.

The problem

  • Long wait times until a correct diagnosis by a specialist.
  • Uncertain diagnosis when not with a specialist.
  • Stress increases as well as confusion during testing
  • Developing countries have longer wait times.

Making human life better

  • Saving lives by reducing diagnosis time.
  • Improving breast cancer diagnosis overall
  • Lowering stress levels for women and men.

Solution methodology

  • Using convolutional neural network (CNN) written in python it can help ahieve better diagnoses of tumors.
  • Can help guide medical practitioner and radiological technicians achieve better diagnosis
  • Accuracy of 90% and can improve with more data.
  • Uncertain Results are indicated and 100% certain.
